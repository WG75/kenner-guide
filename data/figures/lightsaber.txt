Accessory Name: Lightsaber (Kenner Vintage)

Category:
Primary Weapon – Jedi / Sith Figures

--------------------------------------------------

OVERVIEW

Vintage Kenner lightsabers exist in TWO fundamentally different types:

1. Double Telescoping (DT)
2. Single Telescoping

These are NOT variants of the same accessory.
They are separate production systems.

--------------------------------------------------

TYPE 1: DOUBLE TELESCOPING (DT)

- Earliest production only
- Two extending sections (inner + outer blade)
- Extremely rare
- Found mainly on early 12-back figures

Colours:
- Yellow (Luke)
- Red (Vader – mostly pre-production)
- Blue (Ben – extremely rare)

Mould Types:
- M1 (Circled DT)
- M2 (Lettered DT)

Key Traits:
- Very fragile
- Often broken inner blade
- High collector value

--------------------------------------------------

TYPE 2: SINGLE TELESCOPING

- Standard production version
- Replaced DT very early
- Used across entire line

Mould Types:
- M1–M10 (plus subvariants)

Key Traits:
- One extendable blade
- Wide variation by factory
- Much more common

--------------------------------------------------

CRITICAL COLLECTOR RULE

👉 DT sabers are NOT interchangeable with Single sabers  
👉 A DT figure must have a DT saber to be correct

--------------------------------------------------

IDENTIFICATION STRATEGY

First determine:

1. DT or Single?
2. Then identify mould type
3. Then match to figure COO

--------------------------------------------------

COMMON MISTAKES

- Calling DT a “variant” of single
- Matching sabers by colour only
- Ignoring mould differences
- Using reproduction sabers unknowingly

--------------------------------------------------

COLLECTOR INSIGHT

DT = rarity driven  
Single = variation driven
