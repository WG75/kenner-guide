Figure Name: Jawa

Category:
Action Figure

Line:
Star Wars (Kenner, 1978 to 1985)

Overview:
The Jawa is one of the core early Star Wars figures in the original Kenner line. It is especially important to collectors because of the very early vinyl cape release, which was replaced very early in production by the more common cloth cloak version.

What Comes with a Jawa:
A vintage Kenner Jawa typically comes with:
- Jawa Blaster
- Either a vinyl cape or a cloth cloak, depending on production period

Standard Accessory:
The standard weapon accessory is the Jawa Blaster.

Cape / Cloak Guidance:
The key variation is the body covering:

- Vinyl Cape
  - Very early release
  - Brown vinyl
  - No stitching
  - Rare and highly desirable
  - Replaced very early in production

- Cloth Cloak
  - The more common version
  - Fabric garment with hood
  - Visible stitching
  - Seen across the great majority of Jawa figures

Important Collector Note:
Cloth cloaks were also present very early in the line because the change from vinyl cape to cloth cloak happened quickly. That is why vinyl cape Jawas are so rare and valuable.

Identification Note:
For most collectors, the main first step is identifying whether the Jawa has:
- a vinyl cape
- or a cloth cloak

Then the blaster mould and colour can be checked for a more precise match.

Collector Summary:
The Jawa is one of the most iconic early Kenner figures. The vinyl cape version is one of the standout early variants in the vintage Star Wars line, while the cloth cloak version is the standard and far more common release.
