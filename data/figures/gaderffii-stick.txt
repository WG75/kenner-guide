Accessory Name: Gaffi Stick (Tusken Raider)

Category:
Primary Weapon – Unique Character Weapon

Used With:
- Tusken Raider (Kenner)
- Tusken Raider (regional variants: PBP, Lili Ledy, etc.)

--------------------------------------------------

OVERVIEW

The Gaffi Stick is the signature weapon of the Tusken Raider and a unique accessory within the Kenner Star Wars line.

It exists in multiple mould families and colour variations, with distinct structural differences used for identification.

--------------------------------------------------

CRITICAL COLLECTOR NOTE

👉 Gaffi Stick = multi-mould accessory  
👉 Identification depends on mould + structure + colour  
👉 Subtle differences matter for correct pairing

--------------------------------------------------

MAIN MOULD FAMILIES

M1 – EARLY MOULD

Key Traits:
- Visible gate scar on shaft
- Slightly sharper edges
- Cleaner sculpt overall
- Defined axe head shape

Colours:
- Brown
- Reddish brown
- Slight tonal variation

Notes:
- Early production
- Often shows clearer mould definition

Quick ID:
👉 Visible gate scar + cleaner sculpt = M1

--------------------------------------------------

M2 – STANDARD PRODUCTION

Key Traits:
- Slightly softer detail
- More uniform shaft
- Reduced sharpness compared to M1

Colours:
- Brown
- Mid brown
- Reddish tones

Notes:
- Most common variant
- Produced across multiple factories

Quick ID:
👉 Balanced shape + softer detail = M2

--------------------------------------------------

M3 – LATER / SIMPLIFIED MOULD

Key Traits:
- Softer sculpt overall
- Less defined axe head
- Slightly thicker shaft
- Reduced detail

Colours:
- Brown
- Dark brown
- Grey-brown tones

Notes:
- Later production
- More cost-efficient tooling

Quick ID:
👉 Chunkier + softer + less detail = M3

--------------------------------------------------

STRUCTURAL VARIATION

Key identification points:

- Axe head shape (sharp vs rounded)
- Shaft thickness
- Handle curvature
- Gate scar presence and position

--------------------------------------------------

COLOUR VARIATION

Observed colours:

- Light brown
- Mid brown
- Reddish brown
- Dark brown
- Grey-brown

IMPORTANT:
👉 Colour variation is factory-driven, not just ageing

--------------------------------------------------

FACTORY ASSOCIATION

KADER:
- Early and mid production
- Stronger brown tones

PBP (Spain):
- Slight variation in finish
- Subtle structural differences

LILI LEDY (Mexico):
- Can show lighter or slightly different tones
- Sometimes softer mould appearance

--------------------------------------------------

IDENTIFICATION STRATEGY

1. Check axe head:
   - Sharp and defined = early
   - Rounded and soft = later

2. Check shaft:
   - Slim vs thicker

3. Look for gate scar:
   - Presence helps identify early moulds

4. Assess detail level:
   - Sharp vs soft

5. Use colour as secondary confirmation

--------------------------------------------------

MATCHING TO FIGURES

- Early Tusken Raider → more likely M1
- Mid production → M2
- Later production → M3

Regional figures:
- May require closer factory matching

--------------------------------------------------

COMMON MISTAKES

- Treating all Gaffi Sticks as identical
- Matching by colour only
- Ignoring mould detail differences
- Using repro accessories unknowingly

--------------------------------------------------

REPRODUCTION WARNING

Repro Gaffi Sticks often:

- Have incorrect proportions
- Lack fine sculpt detail
- Use incorrect plastic tone
- Appear too smooth or too clean

--------------------------------------------------

COLLECTOR INSIGHT

- More variation than most collectors realise
- Correct mould matching improves figure accuracy
- Subtle structural differences are key
