Figure Name: Luke Skywalker

Category:
Action Figure

Line:
Star Wars (Kenner, 1978 to 1984)

Overview:
Luke Skywalker is one of the core figures in the original Kenner Star Wars line and remained in circulation across multiple cardback eras. He is one of the most important figures in the line due to his early lightsaber variants, especially the rare Double Telescoping (DT) version.

Core Collector Note:
Luke Skywalker was one of the first four figures released by Kenner through the Early Bird Certificate Package in early 1978.

The first four Early Bird figures were:
- Luke Skywalker
- Princess Leia Organa
- Chewbacca
- R2-D2

Early Bird Significance:
The Early Bird Luke is especially important because it came with the original arm-fed lightsaber featuring a double-telescoping tip. Kenner quickly removed the extra extending tip because it was too fragile and easily broken.

Accessory Guidance:
Luke Skywalker is primarily associated with early lightsaber variants:
- Double Telescoping lightsaber (very early and rare)
- Single Telescoping / standard early arm-fed lightsaber
- Later mould and factory differences depending on COO and production period

Collector Importance:
The Early Bird Luke is one of the standout early variants in the vintage Kenner line and is one of the most desirable loose figure and accessory combinations for collectors.
