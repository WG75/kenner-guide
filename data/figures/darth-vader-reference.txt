Figure Name: Darth Vader

Character Overview:
Darth Vader is a central character in the Star Wars franchise. Originally Anakin Skywalker, he becomes a Sith Lord under Emperor Palpatine. He first appeared in Star Wars (1977). Portrayed physically by David Prowse and voiced by James Earl Jones.

Collector Names / Aliases:
Vader, Dark Vader

Release Information:
- Released: 1978–1985
- Appears on: Star Wars, Empire Strikes Back, Return of the Jedi, POTF cardbacks
- Early Bird mail-away figure (1978)
- Part of original 12 Back figures

ACCESSORIES

Primary Accessories:

- Saber type depends heavily on factory and production period

--------------------------------------------------

LIGHTSABERS (RED)

Mould Variants:

M1 – Flat transition, lettered hilt (early Kader, Unitoy, Smile, Taiwan)
M2 – Flat transition, unlettered (Unitoy up to 21-back)
M3 – Flat transition, thin tip (Unitoy post 21-back, also Lili Ledy Retorno)
M4 – Flat transition, short shaft (Takara)
M5 – Round transition, wide shaft (Kader & Smile post 12c-back, also PBP)
M6 – Round transition, thin shaft (PBP)
M7 – Smooth transition, EPM to handle (Taiwan)
M8 – Smooth transition, fat tip, long shaft (Glasslite)
M9 – Smooth transition, fat tip, short shaft (Lili Ledy Regreso)
M10 – Smooth transition, EPM to shaft (Top Toys)

Notes:
- 10 main moulds + subvariants
- Produced in multi-cavity mould trees (up to ~40 cavity marks)
- Letter stamping A–Z and AA–NN observed

Timeline Behaviour:
- Early: M1
- Mid: M2 / M3
- Later: M5 dominant for Kader/Smile
- Regional:
  - Taiwan → M7
  - Glasslite → M8
  - Lili Ledy → M9
  - Top Toys → M10

--------------------------------------------------

VINYL CAPES

General Notes:
- 18 main cape variants + subvariants
- PVC material, cut under tension → natural variation
- No true front/back, only left/right orientation

KADER (M1–M5)
- Symmetrical shape
- Rounded collar
- Misaligned armholes common

UNITOY (M6–M11)
- Bulgy shape
- Narrow armholes (often split)

TAIWAN (M12)
- No crosshatch
- Small armholes
- Large collar

GLASSLITE (M13)
- Smooth, glossy
- Symmetrical bulges

TOP TOYS (M14)
- Narrow shape
- Leathery texture

TAKARA (M15)
- Very wide
- Mixed surface texture

LILI LEDY (M16)
- Rounded edges
- No crosshatch

POCH (M17)
- Large armholes
- Rough cut edges

PBP (M18)
- Rounded edges
- Multiple surface patterns

Reissues:
- 1995 Kenner: similar to Kader M2
- 2019 Retro: oversized, smooth

KADER PRODUCTION

COO Family 1:
- Full mixing of torso and head moulds
- Colours: light orange → dark red
- Paired with M1 or M5 sabers

Kader China (No COO):
- Only M3 head
- Later production
- Colours: dark orange → dark red
- Paired with M5

COO Family 2:
- Early production
- Mostly M1 head
- Paired with M1 sabers

COO Family 3:
- Full mixing again
- Paired with M1 and M5

COO Family 3 No COO / Glasslite:
- Only M3 head
- Glasslite used M2 torso only
- Traits:
  - No white belt detail
  - Unique saber + cape
  - Bright red chest variation
