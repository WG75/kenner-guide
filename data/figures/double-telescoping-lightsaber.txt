Accessory Name: Double Telescoping Lightsaber (DT)

Category:
Primary Weapon – Early Kenner Line

Used With:
- Luke Skywalker
- Darth Vader (rare)
- Ben Kenobi (extremely rare)

--------------------------------------------------

OVERVIEW

The Double Telescoping Lightsaber is the earliest lightsaber type released by Kenner. It features two extending sections and was quickly discontinued due to fragility and production issues.

--------------------------------------------------

MAIN MOULD TYPES

M1 – Circled DT
- Unknown factory
- Earliest version
- Often found with Unitoy Luke
- Inner blade frequently broken

Colours:
- Yellow
- Red (mostly pre-production)

--------------------------------------------------

M2 – Lettered DT

- Produced in multiple mould cavities
- Letters stamped on hilt
- Found with Kader, Smile, Unitoy

Colours:
- Pale yellow
- Red (rare)
- Pale blue (very rare)

--------------------------------------------------

KEY TRAITS

- Two-stage extension
- Very fragile
- Inner blade often missing
- Slightly softer plastic

--------------------------------------------------

PRODUCTION NOTE

DT production was stopped early.
Moulds were later reworked into single telescoping sabers.

--------------------------------------------------

IDENTIFICATION

Check:
1. Two extending sections
2. Hilt markings (lettered vs circled)
3. Inner blade condition

--------------------------------------------------

COLLECTOR INSIGHT

- One of the most valuable accessories in the line
- Correct pairing massively impacts figure value
