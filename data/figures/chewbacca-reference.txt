Figure Name: Chewbacca

Character Overview:
Chewbacca (Chewie) is a Wookiee from Kashyyyk, standing approximately 7.5 feet tall. He is the co-pilot of the Millennium Falcon and loyal companion to Han Solo. He carries a bowcaster and speaks Shyriiwook. Portrayed by Peter Mayhew in the original trilogy.

Collector Names / Aliases:
Chewbacca, Chewy, Chewie, OG, O.G., Original

Release Information:
- Released: 1978–1984
- Appears on: Star Wars, Empire Strikes Back, Return of the Jedi cardbacks
- Part of Early Bird mail-away (1978 shipment) M2 Kader Version
- Part of original 12 Back figures

UNITOY (M1) – MADE IN HONG KONG

Variants:
- Light yellow-brown (hard pouch paint)
- Mid yellow-brown (hard pouch paint)
- Mid brown (soft pouch paint, dark blue pupils)
- Mid red-brown (soft pouch paint)
- Dark brown (soft pouch paint)
- Dark green-brown (hard pouch paint)
- Matt black (hard pouch paint)
- Black (hard pouch paint, dot missing)

Notes:
- Early Bird release
- Produced through SW era into ESB (up to ~41 back)
- Wide pouch colour variation
- Early pouch paint sprayed (soft edges), later brushed (hard edges)
- “Dot” detail sometimes painted over
- Known defect: swollen large toe (left leg)

Key Traits:
- Yellow-brown plastic
- Small light/dark blue pupils
- Pouch colour varies widely

Accessory Pairing:
- M2 Unitoy blue-black (SW / early ESB)
- M2 Unitoy blue (mid ESB)

--------------------------------------------------

POCH (MIHK – Unitoy Parts)

Variants:
- Light brown (soft pouch)
- Mid brown (soft pouch)
- Dark brown (hard pouch)
- Very dark brown (hard pouch)
- Matt black (hard pouch, dot)
- Satin black (hard pouch, dot)

Notes:
- Assembled using Unitoy parts
- Some exported outside Spain
- Mixed part figures exist

Key Traits:
- Light yellow-brown plastic
- Large turquoise eyes (sometimes overpainted)
- Melt marks common
- Plastic burrs from sprue removal
- Weak sonic weld (torso separation risk)

Accessory Pairing:
- M2 Unitoy blue (primary)
- Possible M2 blue-black or M1 Kader due to mixing

--------------------------------------------------

PBP (NO COO)

Variants:
- Mid grey-brown pouch (light blue pupils)
- Mid red-brown pouch (mid blue pupils)
- Dark brown pouch (mid blue pupils)
- Very dark brown pouch (mid blue pupils)

Notes:
- Widely exported via Palitoy
- Often missing COO/date markings
- Known toe defect
- Unique large circle under left foot (cavity feature)

Key Traits:
- Reddish-brown plastic
- Large eyes, small pupils
- Melt marks common
- Glossy pouch variation

Accessory Pairing:
- Smile M3 black-blue
- Kader M1 black-blue

KADER (HONG KONG)

Variants:
- Light yellow-brown pouch / red-brown torso
- Mid brown pouch / yellow-brown torso
- Mid yellow-brown pouch / red-brown torso
- Degraded green limbs (NOT a variant)
- Grey-brown pouch (painted dot)

Notes:
- Early Bird and 12 back release
- Sporadic ESB production, later returns in ROTJ/POTF
- Plastic prone to green discolouration
- Toe defect present

Key Traits:
- Reddish or yellow-brown plastic
- Small mid-blue pupils
- Mid-range pouch colours

Accessory Pairing:
- M1 Kader black-blue (through entire run)

--------------------------------------------------

POCH (KADER PARTS – “PURPLE POUCH”)

Variants:
- Purple-brown pouch
- Mixed part examples

Notes:
- Built mainly from Kader parts
- Strong discolouration issues
- Storage conditions affected plastic

Key Traits:
- Purple-brown pouch (distinct Poch trait)
- Greenish limb degradation common
- Weak sonic weld

Accessory Pairing:
- M1 Kader blue-black
- Possible M2 Unitoy blue-black

KADER POTF (NO COO) / GLASSLITE
