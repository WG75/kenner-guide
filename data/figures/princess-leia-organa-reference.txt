Figure Name: Princess Leia Organa

Character Overview:
Princess Leia Organa is a central character in the Star Wars franchise. Introduced in Star Wars (1977), she is a princess of Alderaan, a member of the Imperial Senate, and a leader in the Rebel Alliance. She plays a key role in the destruction of the Death Star and later helps defeat the Empire. Portrayed by Carrie Fisher.

Collector Names / Aliases:
Leia, White Leia, The Princess, Organa, OG, O.G., Original, First 12, 12 Back Leia

Release Information:
- Released: 1978–1984
- Appears on: Star Wars, Empire Strikes Back, Return of the Jedi cardbacks
- Early Bird mail-away figure (shipped spring 1978) M2 Unitoy Version
- Part of original Wave A 12 Back figures

Material Notes:
- Made from rolled and milled PVC sheet
- Shrinkage can occur over time or with heat exposure
- Capes likely die-cut under tension, causing natural size variation

Collector Notes:
