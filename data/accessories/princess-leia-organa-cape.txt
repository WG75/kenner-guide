Accessory Name: Princess Leia Organa Cape

Category:
Soft Goods – Vinyl Cape

Used With:
- Princess Leia Organa (Kenner)
- Regional variants (Lili Ledy, PBP, Glasslite, etc.)

--------------------------------------------------

OVERVIEW

The Princess Leia cape is a white vinyl soft goods accessory included with the Leia Organa figure.

Unlike many assume, this cape exists in multiple clearly defined mould variants (M1–M8), each with distinct physical traits.

These variations are important for accurate figure pairing and advanced collecting.

--------------------------------------------------

CRITICAL COLLECTOR NOTE

👉 Leia cape is NOT a single standard item  
👉 There are multiple mould variants (M1–M8)  
👉 Differences are structural, not just ageing  

--------------------------------------------------

MATERIAL

- Thin vinyl (plastic-like)
- Flexible but prone to creasing
- Can stiffen and yellow with age

--------------------------------------------------

CORE IDENTIFICATION FEATURES

Across all variants, examine:

1. Overall shape (top curve)
2. Hole size and placement
3. Hole spacing
4. Edge cut and finish
5. Vinyl thickness
6. Surface texture (smooth vs slightly grainy)

--------------------------------------------------

MOULD VARIANTS

The Leia cape is categorised into multiple mould families:

-----------------------------------

M1 (KADER)

- Early production
- Distinct hole placement and spacing
- Clean cut edges
- Often associated with early Leia figures

-----------------------------------

M2

- Slight variation from M1
- Differences in hole positioning and curvature
- Subtle but distinct when compared side-by-side

-----------------------------------

M3 (LILI LEDY)

- Typically associated with Lili Ledy production
- Slightly different vinyl tone
- Variation in cut and finish
- Often shows regional production traits

-----------------------------------

M4 (UNITOY)

- Unitoy-linked production
- Variation in top curve and hole alignment
- Slightly different material feel

-----------------------------------

M5

- Minor structural differences from M4
- Hole spacing and shape variation

-----------------------------------

M6

- Further refinement of previous moulds
- Slight changes in edge cut and curvature

-----------------------------------

M7

- Later-stage production variant
- Subtle differences in vinyl and shape

-----------------------------------

M8 (GLASSLITE / PBP)

- Regional variants
- Can show:
  - Different vinyl tone
  - Slightly rougher finish
  - Less consistent cut quality

--------------------------------------------------

COLOUR

Original colour:

- White / off-white

Observed variations:

- Cream tone
- Yellowing (age-related)

IMPORTANT:
👉 Yellowing is usually ageing, NOT a variant indicator

--------------------------------------------------

HOLE DETAILS

- Two neck holes
- Variations include:
  - Spacing differences
  - Hole size differences
  - Clean vs rough punch edges

--------------------------------------------------

CUT & SHAPE

Key variation area:

- Top curve profile differs between moulds
- Some more rounded, others slightly flatter
- Edge trimming varies by factory

--------------------------------------------------

FACTORY ASSOCIATIONS

General tendencies (not absolute):

- Kader → M1
- Lili Ledy → M3
- Unitoy → M4+
- Glasslite / PBP → later / regional variants

--------------------------------------------------

IDENTIFICATION STRATEGY

1. Compare top curve shape  
2. Check hole spacing and alignment  
3. Look at edge cut quality  
4. Assess vinyl thickness and feel  
5. Compare against known M1–M8 references  

--------------------------------------------------

COMMON MISTAKES

- Assuming all Leia capes are identical  
- Confusing yellowing with variant differences  
- Ignoring mould family classification  
- Not matching cape to correct COO figure  

--------------------------------------------------

REPRODUCTION WARNING

Repro Leia capes often show:

- Incorrect hole placement  
- Wrong vinyl thickness  
- Bright white colour with no ageing  
- Poor edge finishing  
- Incorrect shape profile  

--------------------------------------------------

COLLECTOR INSIGHT

- Leia cape is one of the most overlooked variant accessories  
- Correct pairing with figure increases collector value  
- Advanced collectors actively track M1–M8 variants
