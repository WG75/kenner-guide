Accessory Name: Jawa Blaster

Category:
Primary Weapon – Small Blaster

Used With:
- Jawa (standard Kenner release - dark blue)
- Princess Leia Organa (Glasslite variant – silver version)
- Han Solo (Glasslite variant – black version)

--------------------------------------------------

OVERVIEW

The Jawa Blaster is a small, compact blaster pistol included with the Jawa figure. It exists in multiple mould variations and colour forms depending on factory and production stage.

There are three main mould types:

- M1 (Unitoy)
- M2 (Kader / Kader China / Glasslite)
- M3 (Kader)

--------------------------------------------------

MOULD TYPES

M1 – UNITOY

Key Traits:
- Long “bump” on the rear section
- Visible gate scar near handle
- Slightly rougher finish due to mould wear
- has a distinctive bump at the bottom of the handle 

Colours:
- black-blue (semi translucent blueish)
- black-blue (semi translucent greenish)
- black-blue (semi translucent greyish)

Notes:
- Early production mould
- Often shows wear and surface texture variation

--------------------------------------------------

M2 – KADER / CHINA / GLASSLITE

Key Traits:
- Shorter rear bump than M1
- Cleaner, sharper mould detail
- More consistent finish
- Scar to the top right of the bump

Colours:
- black-blue (semi translucent greyish)
- dark-blue (translucent blueish)
- dark-blue (translucent greenish)
- silver (GLASSLITE)
- black (GLASSLITE)


Notes:
- Most commonly found mould
- Used across multiple factories
- Glasslite versions include unique colour variants

--------------------------------------------------

M3 – KADER

Key Traits:
- No rear bump
- Simplified shape compared to M1/M2
- Cleaner silhouette

Colours:
- dark-blue (semi-translucent greyish)
- dark-blue (translucent blueish)
- dark-blue (translucent greenish)

Notes:
- Later production mould
- Less variation than M2

--------------------------------------------------

IDENTIFICATION STRATEGY

To identify correctly:

1. Check rear bump:
   - Long = M1
   - Short = M2
   - None = M3

2. Check plastic tone:
   - Semi-translucent blue = early production
   - Solid darker tones = later runs

3. Check surface texture:
   - Rough = earlier / worn moulds
   - Clean = later production

--------------------------------------------------

GLASSLITE VARIANTS

Glasslite Jawa blasters differ from Kenner versions:

- Can appear in silver or black
- Often paired with Glasslite figures
- Same mould family as M2 but visually distinct colours

--------------------------------------------------

MATCHING TO FIGURES

- Standard Kenner Jawa → can be M1, M2, or M3 depending on figure variants
- Glasslite Leia → silver Jawa blaster
- Glasslite Han Solo → black Jawa blaster

--------------------------------------------------

COMMON MISTAKES

- Assuming all Jawa blasters are identical
- Ignoring mould bump differences
- Matching by colour only
- Confusing Glasslite versions with repros

--------------------------------------------------

COLLECTOR INSIGHT

This is a “small accessory” but high importance piece:

- Correct mould + colour pairing increases value
- Glasslite versions are especially collectible
- All originals float in water (but this is not a guarantee its original) 
- All originals have the gate scar under the handle 

--------------------------------------------------

REPRODUCTION WARNING

Reproduction Jawa blasters exist.

Watch for:
- Incorrect plastic shine
- Too-clean mould lines
- Wrong colour tone
- Lack of correct bump definition
- Sinks when placed in water
