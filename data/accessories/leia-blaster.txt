Accessory Name: Princess Leia Blaster

Category:
Primary Weapon – Small Blaster

Used With:
- Princess Leia Organa (Kenner)
- Princess Leia Organa (Glasslite – Brazil)

--------------------------------------------------

OVERVIEW

Princess Leia does not have a uniquely sculpted blaster in the vintage Kenner line.

Instead, she is issued a small blaster that shares the same base mould family as the Jawa blaster, but must be identified and matched correctly depending on factory and region.

--------------------------------------------------

CRITICAL COLLECTOR NOTE

👉 Leia’s blaster is NOT a unique sculpt  
👉 It is part of the same mould family as the Jawa blaster  
👉 Correct version depends heavily on factory (Kenner vs Glasslite)

--------------------------------------------------

STANDARD KENNER VERSION

Mould:
- Same mould family as Jawa blaster (typically M2 variants)

Traits:
- Compact pistol shape
- Consistent with Jawa-issued blaster sculpt
- Clean mould detail depending on production run

Colours:
- Black
- Dark blue
- Blue-grey

Notes:
- This is the correct pairing for standard Kenner Leia
- No silver version used in standard Kenner production

--------------------------------------------------

GLASSLITE (BRAZIL) VERSION

Mould:
- Same base mould as Jawa blaster

Traits:
- Often softer detail
- Slightly different plastic finish
- Can appear lighter in tone

Colours:
- Silver (key identifier)
- Occasionally lighter plastic tones

Notes:
- Silver Leia blaster is specific to Glasslite production
- Often confused with incorrect pairings or repros

--------------------------------------------------

IDENTIFICATION STRATEGY

1. Identify figure origin:
   - Kenner → standard dark blaster
   - Glasslite → possible silver blaster

2. Check colour:
   - Black / dark blue → Kenner
   - Silver → Glasslite only

3. Confirm mould:
   - Should match Jawa blaster mould family

--------------------------------------------------

MATCHING TO FIGURES

Kenner Leia:
- Black / dark blue small blaster (Jawa mould type)

Glasslite Leia:
- Silver Jawa-style blaster

--------------------------------------------------

COMMON MISTAKES

- Assuming all Leia blasters are silver
- Assigning Glasslite accessories to Kenner figures
- Treating Leia blaster as a unique sculpt
- Matching by colour without checking origin

--------------------------------------------------

REPRODUCTION WARNING

Repro Leia blasters often:

- Use incorrect plastic tone
- Have overly smooth finish
- Lack proper mould definition

Silver repros are especially common

--------------------------------------------------

COLLECTOR INSIGHT

- Leia’s blaster is simple, but often mismatched
- Correct factory pairing matters more than mould variation
- Glasslite versions are distinct and should be treated separately
