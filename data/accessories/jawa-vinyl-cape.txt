Accessory Name: Jawa Vinyl Cape

Category:
Clothing / Soft Goods

Used By:
Jawa (very early production)

Description:
The vinyl cape is the original body covering included with the earliest Jawa figures. It is made from thin brown vinyl (plastic), not fabric.

Key Characteristics:
- Smooth plastic material
- No stitching
- Holds its shape more than fabric
- Slightly rigid compared to cloth

Collector Notes:
This version was replaced very early in production by the cloth cloak. Because of that short production window, genuine vinyl capes are rare and highly valuable.

Common Confusion:
Many reproductions exist. Authentic examples show natural ageing, slight warping, and correct cut shape. Condition and authenticity are critical when assessing value.
